  • Interactive Tour of Go: https://tour.golang.org/

  • How to name variables:

  • Understand Concurrency: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cN_DpYBzKso
  • Go Proverbs - Rob Pike: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PAAkCSZUG1c
